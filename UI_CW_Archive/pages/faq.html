FAQ

Click on "New Task List +" on the left hand sidebar. This will display the New List form, enter the Name and Deadline for the List.

Open the list you wish to modify by clicking the button on the left sidebar. Click "Modify" button, this will open the modify form. Change the details and submit.

With the list selected click on "Add Task +". This will display the New task form, enter the Name and Deadline for the Task.

With the list selected click on the task you wish to delete. This will activate the delete task button. Confirm deletion to remove the task.

With the list selected click on the task you wish to delete. click the delete list button. Confirm deletion to remove the task.

With list or event selected click on "Share". Here you can add a new user to share with by entering their email and their permissions. Press save and send to send an email to the new user.

Read Only: Allows another user to view your List or Event but they cannot make any changes.

Modify/Delete: Allows another user full permission to edit and delete an Event or List.

Edit: Allows another user to make changes or add tasks but does not allow them to delete.

Click on the day or select the timeslot you want to add the Event to. A pop-up will ask you what action to take, select "Add Event". A new pop-up will come up that will ask you for event details. Once complete, submit the new event.
